Founder · Next Vision

Paul Said

Entrepreneur. Coach. Storyteller. Born in Tanzania. Building community in Tulsa.

The Story

A Vision Born from
Communal Roots.

Paul Said was born and raised in Tanzania — a country whose culture is built around a profound communal philosophy: the belief that people rise together, not alone. That ethos didn't just shape how Paul sees the world. It became the engine behind everything he builds.

Growing up in Tanzania, Paul was surrounded by a way of life where neighbors were family, resources were shared, and the community's strength was measured by how it uplifted its most overlooked members. When Paul came to the United States, he brought that worldview with him — and never let it go.

"In Tanzania, we didn't just grow up around each other — we grew up because of each other. That's the foundation everything I build is standing on."
— Paul Said, Founder of Next Vision

Before founding Next Vision, Paul built a career moving between worlds. His years in sales sharpened a rare ability to listen, connect, and translate what a person truly needs — not just what they say they want. That skill, embedded in his Tanzanian roots, became his superpower in both coaching and creative work.

Paul's work in soccer coaching gave him a second education in leadership — the kind that happens on a field, not in a boardroom. Managing a team, reading dynamics, building culture, inspiring individuals to perform beyond their own expectations — he carried every one of those lessons into how he leads Next Vision and the Tulsa Film Tribe.

When Paul arrived in Tulsa, he saw something familiar: a city with deep community roots that hadn't yet found its full creative voice. He saw people doing extraordinary things in isolation. He saw stories that deserved to be told. And he saw — in Tulsa's cultural mosaic — the same communal potential he grew up with in Tanzania. Next Vision became his answer.

What Drives Him
Ubuntu — Tanzanian Roots
"I am because we are" — not just an influence on Paul. It's the operating system of Next Vision's entire model.
Soccer Coaching & Leadership
Paul's coaching background taught him how to build a winning team culture from scratch — seeing potential in individuals and channeling it toward a shared goal.
Sales & Human Connection
Years in sales gave Paul a deep understanding of trust — how it's built, how it's broken, and why it's the only real currency in community-rooted work.
Film as the Flagship
At the heart of Next Vision's ambition is a national film project — Paul's most personal expression of what communal storytelling can become at its highest form.
